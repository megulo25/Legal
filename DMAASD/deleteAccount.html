Steps to Delete Your DMAASD Account

1. Log into the DMAASD app

2. Navigate to the "Settings" screen through the bottom tab labeled "Settings"

3. On the bottom of the screen you'll see text that says "Delete my account". It's located underneath the version number.

4. Tap on that text and you'll be prompted to confirm your decision for deleting the account.

5. Then your account will be deleted along with all of your data and you'll be logged out of the app.
